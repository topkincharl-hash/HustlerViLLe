QueenZoe Remote Dominion Architecture

Mission

The Remote Dominion System extends QueenZoe's sovereign operating environment beyond the local device, allowing the Queen to project intelligence, execution, and orchestration capabilities across distributed Linux infrastructure.

Rather than functioning as a conventional SSH client, the Remote Dominion transforms remote systems into active execution domains governed by the Queen Kernel.
